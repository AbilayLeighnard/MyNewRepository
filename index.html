A TREE

I

think

That I

shall never see

a poem as lovely as a tree

A tree whose hungry mouth

is pressed against the earth`s sweet

flowing breast .A tree that looks at God

all day and lifts her leafy hands to pray

A tree that may in summer wear a nest of robins

in her hair , upon whose bossom snow has lain

who intimately live in the rain

Poems

are made

by

fools

like

me,

but

only

God

can

make a tree
